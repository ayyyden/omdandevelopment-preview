Frequently Asked Questions

Will my HOA approve turf?

We follow Heritage Lake, Audie Murphy Ranch, and Sun City specs—proper base depth, approved blades, and a photo packet for your submission.

We spec lighter turfs, cooling infill, and optional shade sails so patios and play spaces stay comfortable.
