Palm Springs plumbing FAQ

Can you work between guest stays?

Yes. Share your turnover window and we’ll stage parts so the water is restored, finishes are buttoned up, and the home is cleaned before the next arrival.

Do you preserve exposed plumbing fixtures?

We catalog finishes, source matching trim, and fabricate custom escutcheons when a mid-century part is discontinued.

What about mineral-heavy water?

We install filtration, softening, or scale-control systems and program maintenance reminders so fixtures stay clean longer.
